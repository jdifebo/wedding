About Us

Paige and Joe met through mutual friends during their freshman year at the University of Illinois. Joe was impressed that Paige was taking 20 hours of classes. Paige thought Joe was weird and nerdy. They started hanging out more, and Joe worked up the courage to ask Paige out. Paige planned on saying no, but instead realized that she actually liked Joe quite a bit. They started dating and became "Facebook official" on October 11, 2009.

Paige finished her degree in chemistry, and started graduate school at the University of Michigan. Joe finished his degree in computer science, and stayed to get a master's degree at Illinois. He then moved to Ann Arbor to join Paige, where he now works as a software developer. Paige plans to complete her Ph.D. during the summer of 2017.

Joe proposed to Paige on August 2, 2016 while on vacation in Michigan's beautiful upper peninsula. They don't know what life has in store for them next, but they are very excited to face it together, surrounded by the love and support of their friends and families.
